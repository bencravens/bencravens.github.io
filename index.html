Welcome to my website

This is the personal website of Ben Cravens. My background is in physics and computational modelling. At the University of Otago, I completed my BSc Hons (First Class) in Physics in 2019. During my undergraduate degree, I worked as a research assistant for the physics department every summer, focusing on computational modelling topics. I conducted research on topics such as climate models and bayesian inference algorithms.

At the end of my research career, I had job opportunities where I could apply my computational modelling skills to a career in data science, but decided not to out of a concern for the ethical issues this profession faces.

Instead I am currently retraining in Engineering.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading (especially science-fiction and fantasy), martial arts (mostly BJJ), playing the guitar, board games, and hiking.
